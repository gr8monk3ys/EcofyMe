What is this?

Our goal was to make a web app that made cleaning up the community a communal effort. This is a project submitted for the CruzHacks 2020 event.

Who are we?

We are four UC Merced students that banded together to make this project a reality in only 36 hours.

Why do we care?

Seeing trash on the side of the road can be common nowadays. Increasingly we see rubbish invading places that it does not belong in and this is why we decided to make this web app. Through this app, we hope to make the process of cleaning up this unwanted garbage a fun, communal effort that rewards participants with points. For now, the points are meaningless but future plans involve backing these points up with monetary value of some sort.
